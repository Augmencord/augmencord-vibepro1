Feel of Antigravity:


What is this Antigravity? A Super intelligent framework that has many artificial intelligent neural networks(for the ease just consider them as artificial brains) inside. To simplify it further, a manager who handles a team of a few people.


Think of a cricket captain who has played with the same team for years. He knows exactly who to turn to in different situations because he understands every player's strengths, weaknesses, and style of play.He relies on what he knows about his players and puts the right person in the right role at the right time.


But, Antigravity is a super intelligent captain, a super intelligent manager.


Suppose your team is defending 160 in a T20. The super intelligent captain doesn't sit down and work everything out alone. He will have a word with the senior most bowler about who should bowl the death overs, ask the wicketkeeper what he is noticing about the opposition batters, check with the spinner about how much the pitch is turning, and maybe even get input from the coach on field placements. Everyone contributes a piece of the puzzle. The captain listens to all of it, keeps track of what is happening on the field, and then makes the final call.


Antigravity has the same feel. If i talk about building a website, it feels like you are building it by just talking about it. You don’t need to know coding or how things work inside. You just say something like I want a simple website with a login page and a dashboard, and it starts building it step by step. One part creates the front page, another part connects the data, and another part checks for errors. You don’t have to manage anything or write complex code. You just explain in simple words what you want, and it turns that into a working website. It feels like you are guiding a full web development team, even if you are completely new.
